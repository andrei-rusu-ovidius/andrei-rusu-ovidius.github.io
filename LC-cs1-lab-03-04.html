Laboratory 03-04

Mathematical and Computational Logic

Speciality:

  • Informatică, seria 1, anul 1;
  • Computer Science, anul 1.

Teacher:

  • lect. dr. Andrei Rusu

Obiectives:

  • To build the minimal normal forms for the formulas of the clasic propositional calculus.
  • To design and to implement the algorithm for obtaining minimal normal forms for propositional formulas.

Conditions for solving the laboratory tasks

  1. If the formula F is a tautology then consider its conjunction with the formula ((A -> B) v (B ~ C) v (C -> D)).
  2. If the formula F is a contradiction then consider its disjunction with the formula ((A -> B) v (B ~ C) v (C -> D)).
  3. If the formula F contains less than 4 distinct propositional variables, then consider its conjunction with the formula ((A -> B) v (B ~ C) v (C -> D)).
  4. In the following we consider the formula F is the last formula obtained as a result of the previous actions.
  5. Every student work at the formula from the list in accordance with the variant given by its number in the list of students:
    • Computer Science,
    • Informatică.
  6. Any programming language can be used, for example, C, C++, Java, Prolog, Lisp, Python, PHP, etc.
  7. Any program should state clearly:
    • initial conditions,
    • which is the result,
    • important intermediary results on the way to the main results.

Tasks:

  1. Build the truth table of the formula F. (1p)
  2. Build the conjunctive normal form as well as the disjunctive normal form of the formula F. (2p)
  3. Build the minimal disjunctive normal form of F. (2p)
  4. Build the minimal conjunctive normal form of F. (2p)
  5. Design and program the algorithms for above mentioned tasks. (rest of the points -:) )
